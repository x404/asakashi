About us

The history of Asakashi starts in 1994 as an engineering company. Research group was developing a range of filtration products with innovative designs leading production brands.


The first filtration product of own manufacturing was appeared in 2006. It was spin on, cabin and air filters. All items were suitable for Japanese and Korean vehicle range only. Entire production process goes under quality control of Asaka-Shi Research Lab, Inc.

History of the company

2006

The first filtration product of own manufacturing was appeared in 2006

2007

JS Asakashi starts production of Fuel injectors

2009

The company expands its range with element oil and element fuel filters. This new range covers vehicles with cartridge filtration which allows replacement of only filter elements and seals during service, while the cartages and other components are not required to be changed for the whole lifespan of the vehicle. It makes filter service works be easier, cheaper and more environmentally friendly.

2010

Transmission filters introduced into JS Range and becomes a “calling card” for Asakashi. JS becomes one of the few companies with this product on the market.

2013

JS Asakashi releases its first in-tank fuel filter range
